Singapore
Tour Package
Day 1: Explore the City

Morning
Start your day with a visit to Marina Bay Sands. Enjoy the stunning views of the city from the observation deck.

Afternoon
Explore Gardens by the Bay, a beautiful garden featuring the iconic Supertree Grove and Flower Dome.

Evening
Head to Chinatown for dinner. Explore the vibrant streets, visit Sri Mariamman Temple, and try local street food.

Day 2: Sentosa Island

Morning
Take a morning stroll along Sentosa's beaches. Visit Universal Studios Singapore for a day of entertainment and rides.

Afternoon
Have lunch at one of the restaurants in Sentosa. Explore S.E.A. Aquarium and Adventure Cove Waterpark.

Evening
Enjoy the light and water show, Wings of Time, at Sentosa.

China
Tour Package
Day 1: Beijing - Historical Wonders

Morning
Visit Tiananmen Square, the world's largest public square. Explore the Forbidden City, a UNESCO World Heritage site with a rich history.

Afternoon
Have lunch in a local restaurant. Walk or cycle through the historic Hutongs to experience traditional Beijing culture.

Evening
Enjoy a Peking Duck dinner, a famous Beijing dish.

Day 2: Great Wall Adventure

Morning
Head to the Mutianyu section of the Great Wall of China. Spend the morning hiking and exploring this iconic structure.

Afternoon
Have lunch near the Great Wall. Visit the Summer Palace, a beautiful royal garden.

Evening
Return to the city and explore Wangfujing Street for shopping and street food.
